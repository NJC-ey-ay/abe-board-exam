TABLE OF SPECIFICATIONS OF SUBJECTS IN LICENSURE EXAMINATION FOR 
AGRICULTURAL AND BIOSYSTEMS ENGINEERS
I. General Objectives
1.
To summarize the general scope and coverage of the Licensure Examination for Agricultural 
and Biosystems Engineers which shall serve as guide to pass the written technical 
examination in the following subjects as required by Law:
a)
Agricultural and Biosystems Power, Energy and Machinery Engineering (18%);
b)
Land and Water Resources Engineering (18%);
c)
Agricultural and Biosystems Structures and Environment Engineering (18%);
d)
Agricultural and Bioprocess Engineering (18%);
e)
Project Management, Feasibility Study Preparation/Evaluation, Research, Development 
sand Extension on Agricultural and Biosystems Engineering (8%);
Fundamentals of Agricultural, Fishery, Ecological and Environmental Sciences (6%);
9)
Mathematics and Basic Engineering Principles (8%); and
h)
Laws, Professional Standards, and Ethics (6%).
2.
This summary of base knowledge and competencies shall constitute the core of the 
examination questions for all areas to be tested. However, this compilation is by no means 
the only guide to the coverage of subjects in the field of agricultural and biosystems 
engineering.
3.
To be able to implement, monitor, and administer the licensure examination.
